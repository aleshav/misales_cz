      Our Love story

      Nov, 2007

      First date

      They first met in a pub (of course) in Denmark in 2007. It took Ales a few months to ask Misa out for a proper date in June the following year. At their first date Ales drove about 100km on a bike, while Misa travelled only 25km. It still remains a miracle how she has done that.

      Aug, 2008

      Vietnam

      Just after they started dating Ales wanted to visit his brother, who was then a missionary of hygiene in Vietnam. Misa has decided that this is the best opportunity to get to know Ales and wanted to go with him. But her father said nobody is going anywhere unless he meet the hairy men in person and pass his test. Ales found the test too easy and got permission to take Misa with him.

      Aug, 2009

      Nottingham

      After Misa finished her studies in Olomouc Ales got a job offer in Derby, UK. They decided to relocate there and moved in together. After a while they even bought their first (and so far their only) car. A Red Ford Focus.

      Apr, 2011

      London

      Nottingham soon became too small for them and when an opportunity arose in London, they took it without hesitation. First they lived in a house with a garden in Nemoure road, but the neighbourhood was pretty feral with a lot of foxes hunting in packs. They prefered moving into a flat with predictable neighbours: funeral directors.

      Jan, 2014

      The Proposal

      For the past 5 years Misa has been expecting a proposal to happen any day, any minute, whilst discretely asking for a date when it would happen. In January 2014 she had given up and was beginning to enjoy the unmarried life with Ales, just as it was meant to be. Imagine her surprise when Ales presented her a ring from one knee.
